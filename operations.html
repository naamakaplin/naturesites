MILITARY OPERATIONS

Israel Defense Forces a large number of military operations, This category
gathers all military operations in chronological order, according to periods.

litany operation

from March 1978, in which the Israeli army entered the southern part of Lebanon up to the Litani River,

the first intifada

a largely violent uprising that began on December 9, 1987, and officially ended with the signing of the Oslo Accords in 1993

the second intifada

"from September 2000 to February 2005, was a violent Palestinian protest uprising against the State of Israel.

Operation Cast Lead

"From December 2008 to January 2009, included airstrikes and ground entry.

Cloud pillar operation

"From November 2012 to November 2012, began with an attack by fighter jets of the Israeli Air Force and the Israeli Navy."

Operation Solid Cliff

"From July 2014 to August 2014, a military operation against Hamas in Gaza that began after three Israeli boys were kidnapped and murdered.

LOperation Wall Guard

"From May 2021 to May 2021, starting after the Palestinian terrorist organizations, led by Hamas, began firing massive rockets at the area surrounding Gaza.

Operation Dawn

"From August 2022 to August 2022, during which the Israeli Air Force attacked a series of targets identified with the Palestinian Islamic Jihad.
